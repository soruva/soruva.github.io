Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Finqly free to use?

Yes! Most features are free. Optional premium features may be offered through one-time purchases or subscriptions.

Do I need to sign up or log in?

No login is required. Finqly is designed to be fully usable without any personal account.

What kind of insights does Finqly provide?

Finqly helps you reflect on your investment emotions and patterns. It offers educational insights, emotion tracking, and a weekly trend forecast.

Is Finqly giving me financial advice?

No. Finqly is not a financial advisor and does not offer investment recommendations. The app is for educational and reflective purposes only.

How is my data stored?

All data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to servers.

Can I delete or reset my records?

Yes. You can reset your saved trends and diagnosis history from the app settings.

Is customer support available?

Finqly is independently developed and does not offer individual support. Most common questions are answered in this FAQ or through in-app help sections.

What if the app crashes or behaves unexpectedly?

Please try restarting the app. If issues persist, ensure you are using the latest version.

How can I access the legal documents?

You can view our Privacy Policy, Terms, and Disclaimer through the app’s settings menu or at our official legal site.

How can I request data removal or opt out?

While Finqly does not collect personal data, you can request deletion of your app history via our Google Form.
