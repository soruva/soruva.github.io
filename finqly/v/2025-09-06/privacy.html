Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how SoruvaLab (“we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information in connection with the Finqly app (“App”). Finqly is designed to work without accounts or sign-in.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII). The App is fully functional without login or registration.

2. Local-Only Storage

All data—such as diagnosis results and emotional history—is stored only on your device (e.g., local storage/SharedPreferences). We do not transmit or back up this data to our servers.

3. In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)

Payments are processed by Google Play. We do not receive your payment card details. The App may receive a purchase token from Google Play to unlock premium features; this token is handled on-device only and is not linked by us to your identity or sent to our servers. For details on Google’s processing, please refer to Google’s privacy policy.

4. No Third-Party Analytics or Ads

Finqly does not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, cookies, or tracking tools.

5. External Links

When you open external links (e.g., help forms or legal pages), they are opened in your browser and governed by the destination site’s privacy policy.

6. Data Deletion

Because all Finqly data is stored locally, uninstalling the App removes the stored data. You can also clear the App’s storage from your device settings. If you wish to contact us about deletion or other privacy requests, you can use our Google Form or email us.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

8. International Users

We aim to respect privacy expectations in applicable jurisdictions (e.g., GDPR/CCPA). Since the App does not collect or transmit personal data, rights such as access or erasure generally do not apply; however, you may still contact us with any questions or requests.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Please check this page periodically for changes.

10. Intellectual Property

11. Prohibited Use

You may not use the App’s insights or interface for commercial purposes, data mining, or reverse engineering. Finqly is provided for personal, non-commercial use only.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Policy, contact us at support@soruvalab.com.
